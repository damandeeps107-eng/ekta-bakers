Our Story

Crafting Sweet Memories in Barnala

Ekta Bakers is a trusted homemade bakery founded and nurtured by Chef Ekta Mehra. Starting our journey in 2019 in Barnala, Punjab, we have dedicated ourselves to the art of creating premium-quality baked goods that taste as spectacular as they look.

We specialize in freshly baked cakes, pastries, and artisanal desserts prepared with love, creativity, and clean ingredients. Without using mass-production techniques or artificial preservatives, every order is handcrafted to ensure customizable luxury tailored to your celebrations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about how to order, eggless variants, freshness, or payments? Find all answers here.

You can easily place an order by clicking our "Order on WhatsApp" buttons. For custom cakes, we recommend using the visual builder form above. Once you submit the form, a structured template will be generated for you to send to Chef Ekta on WhatsApp. We will discuss references, sizes, and finalize details.

For custom cakes and large dessert hampers, we require at least 24 to 48 hours notice. This gives us enough time to prepare specialized toppings, source fresh components, and bake everything to perfection. For urgent orders, please call us directly to check availability.

Yes, absolutely! 100% of our menu (cakes, cupcakes, brownies, cookies, hampers) can be prepared eggless (vegetarian) upon request. We maintain strict separation of baking tools and ingredients to ensure complete dietary safety.

Yes, we offer home delivery across Barnala, Punjab, and select nearby locations. Delivery charges are calculated based on your distance. Alternatively, you can schedule a self-pickup from our home kitchen.

We accept all digital payments including UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), Bank Transfers, and Cash on Pickup/Delivery. For custom cake orders, a 50% advance deposit is required to confirm the booking.
